# Findings & Insights

## Key Discoveries
- **Radiation Dependency:** Solar radiation is the absolute primary driver of energy generation.
- **Cloud Cover Impact:** As cloud cover increases from Low to High, the average power generated significantly decreases.
- **Thermal Efficiency:** Extreme temperatures slightly alter the variance of power generation, suggesting panel thermal dynamics play a role.
- **Data Quality:** Raw telemetry data contains significant noise (negative values, outliers) requiring robust automated cleaning pipelines.
